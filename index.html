Data Warehouse Expertise

Team has designed and implemented the Enterprise Data warehouse for Spirit Airlines, America which serves as a single source of truth for all the reporting needs for the organization. The transactional data from various sources like Navitaire ODS(Booking and Revenue), Sabre(Flight Ops), (CRM), Netracer (Baggage Incident data),Qualtrics(Survey Data),Competitor Data, Search data were integrated for the data to flow into the DWH. The DWH is being used by the reporting layer consisting of Business objects and Power BI to create Dashboards, Reports, Scheduled Reports and tickers meeting various business needs.
